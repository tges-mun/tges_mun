The United Nations Commission on the Status of Women (UNCSW)

The United Nations Commission on the Status of Women (UNCSW) is dedicated to promoting gender equality and the empowerment of women.

It works to advance women's rights globally, addressing issues such as gender-based violence and economic inequality.

Agenda

Empowering women and girls in rural areas.
